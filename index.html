UTTHAN CONCLAVE 2022

About Utthan
We, the Gyansagar family, have always worked towards
bringing a positive change and giving back as much as we can to the society. With great pleasure
we present to you our first ever conclave

world a better place for everyone.

A two day long celebration that’ll include:
✔ Informative and fun Interactive sessions with various
NGOs, an opportunity to know about their work and everything related from them.
✔ Audience interaction, doubt sessions where audience
get a chance to interact with the guests, also resolve all their doubts.
✔ Fun surprise events. And a lot more… Join us in this celebration of humankind!
